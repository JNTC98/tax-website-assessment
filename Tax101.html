Tax 101

A beginner's guide to essential business tax topics. Learn what taxes apply, how to keep records, and when to file so you can stay compliant and avoid penalties.

Key Topics

  • Filing basics: choosing the right forms and understanding filing frequencies.
  • Deductions and allowances: which business expenses reduce taxable income.
  • Important deadlines: staying on schedule for quarterly and annual filings.

Understanding Business Taxes

Most businesses pay taxes on net income after deducting allowable expenses. Tracking income, invoices, and receipts throughout the year makes filing easier and helps you identify savings opportunities.

Depending on your business structure, you may also need to pay payroll taxes, sales tax, or self-employment tax. Getting these basics right builds a stronger financial foundation.
