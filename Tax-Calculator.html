Tax Calculator

Use this page to estimate basic business tax liabilities. Our calculator is designed to help you plan ahead and compare potential tax costs.

How it works

Start with your gross income, then subtract deductions and business expenses that are allowed by tax rules. The calculator estimates your taxable income and shows how much tax you may owe.

This tool is meant for planning only. Use it to prepare for filing season, but always confirm your results with actual tax forms or a qualified tax agent.

Calculator Tips

Include only legitimate business expenses such as rent, utilities, and supplies. Do not add personal spending or non-business costs, and remember that tax laws vary by location.
