About the Project

This website was developed as a digital service concept for business tax support, designed to help users understand what they need to know, where they may be overpaying, and how they can plan more effectively.

The overall objective is to make tax information less intimidating and more practical for everyday business owners.

Design Thinking Approach

Following a human-centred design process, the project begins by understanding the needs of entrepreneurs and small business owners before shaping the content and structure of the experience.

Design thinking is applied as a reflective process of exploration, problem framing, and solution development, while the site itself communicates that logic through a clear information design.

Project Purpose

The service supports users who need guidance on tax returns, filing basics, savings opportunities, and business financial planning. It is especially relevant for people who are new to business ownership or dealing with changing tax rules.

Stakeholders and User Personas

Primary users: new business founders, family business owners, and small operators who want practical tax support without needing to speak to an accountant immediately.

Secondary users: students launching online businesses and established owners managing changing obligations such as GST or import-related costs.

Portfolio Message

What makes this project strong is its balance between business purpose and user experience. It presents a professional service concept that is informative, credible, and designed to help users act with confidence.
