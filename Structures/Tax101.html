Tax 101

Tax 101 is the learning hub of the website, created to break business tax into practical ideas that are easier to understand and act on.

It is designed for people who may not have a finance background but still need clear guidance around filing, deductions, planning, and day-to-day decision-making.

Start with the essentials

  • Income: money earned by the business is the foundation of most tax calculations.
  • Expenses: legitimate business costs can help reduce taxable amounts when recorded properly.
  • Records: good records make filing easier and reduce the risk of mistakes.

Why records matter

Organised records help business owners track what was earned, what was spent, and what needs to be reported. They also make planning far less stressful.

What good planning looks like

Planning ahead means understanding deadlines, preparing money in advance, and reviewing what the business is doing before tax time arrives.

Common beginner questions

  • What should be recorded as a business expense?
  • When do filing obligations usually arise?
  • How can a business prepare without feeling rushed?

The bigger picture

Understanding the basics of tax does more than improve compliance. It helps business owners feel more in control, make stronger decisions, and approach tax as part of everyday business management rather than a sudden burden.
